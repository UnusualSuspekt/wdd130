Purpose

Our purpose is to guide and prepare thrilling and enjoying adventures fit for families and friends alike. Don't know where to start? This is the place to start whether you are new or experienced to river rafting.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) Our target audience is families with children and teens. Preferably, they would be financially stable, or at least stable enough to take trips once in a while. What do they want? Our customers are looking for a place to plan a family trip and learn experience something new. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Our website will be focused toward those on a laptop, and evetually modified to mobile users.
